Buttons

Buttons in Gradient are styled using the button element.

Button types have been redefined, including button, input, submit, and reset.

Buttons come in four sizes. Just use the .lBtn, .nBtn, .sBtn, and .esBtn classes.

You can also style anchors with these classes to turn them into a button.
